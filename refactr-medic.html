Our Services

🫁 Volume render lung image

Your lungs, brought to life in 3D. Using advanced volume rendering technology, we transform raw CT data into a vivid, explorable model — giving you and your doctor a complete picture of your pulmonary health.

🫁 Mesh model lung image

We build a precise digital replica of your anatomy — layer by layer. Our mesh generation technology creates a detailed 3D surface model from your CT scan, making complex structures easy to explore and understand.

🫁 AI tumor detection image

Early detection, clearly visualized. Our AI scans your CT data to identify potential tumors and marks them directly on your 3D model — so you and your doctor can see exactly where they are and how they look.
